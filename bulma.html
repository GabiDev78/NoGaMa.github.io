Bounty Hunter

What is Bounty Hunter ?


Bounty hunter is a video game developped with Unity by NoGaMa group, a group created by four students at EPITA. Bounty Hunter is a game of type MOBA in which players embody combat vehicles in an arena. Each vehicle has his own abilities and sounds. At the project realease, five different "Reapers" are available in the game. The "Reapers" are the names of the characters. For further information on the Reapers, please look at their individual description made on the eponym page.

The game is in 2.5D, which allows us to implement an exclusively isometric vision. The player controls a single vehicle in several game modes (under development), each with several different fighting abilities, such as shots and ballistics. The game is offered with 3 different maps, but we have created a map editor to give you the opportunities to create your own maps and follow your fantasies!

The system that allows us to carry out all these implementations is Unity, a 3D engine specialized in video games. Unity accepts C# and JavaScript languages, two languages that we are more or less familiar with, which have helped us during development.
